ELON MUSK

Elon Musk (born June 28, 1971, Pretoria, South Africa) South African -born American entrepreneur who cofounded the electronic-payment firm PayPal and formed SpaceX, maker of launch vehicles and spacecraft. He was also one of the first significant investors in, as well as chief executive officer of, the electric car manufacturer Tesla.

About the The Great Visionary Entrepreneur and Technologist

☛ Elon Musk was born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa.

☛ He showed entrepreneurial tendencies from a young age, selling his first software, a space game called Blastar, at the age of 12.

☛Musk moved to Canada in his late teens, attending Queen's University before transferring to the University of Pennsylvania, where he earned degrees in both physics and economics.

☛ In 1995, Musk dropped out of a Ph.D. program at Stanford University after just two days to pursue his entrepreneurial ambitions.

☛ He co-founded Zip2, a software company, in 1996, which provided business directories and maps for newspapers. Compaq acquired Zip2 in 1999 for nearly $300 million . With the proceeds from the Zip2 sale, Musk co-founded X.com, an online payment company, in 1999. X.com eventually became PayPal after a merger, and it was acquired by eBay in 2002 for $1.5 billion.

☛ After PayPal, Musk turned his attention to space exploration and founded SpaceX (Space Exploration Technologies Corp.) in 2002, with the goal of reducing space transportation costs and enabling the colonization of mars.

☛ In 2004, Musk founded Tesla Motors (now Tesla, Inc.) with the aim of accelerating the advent of sustainable transport by producing electric vehicles. He is also the founder of SolarCity (now part of Tesla), a solar energy services company, and co-founder of Neuralink, a neurotechnology company focused on developing brain–computer interfaces.

☛ Musk has been involved in various other ventures and projects, including OpenAI, The Boring Company (focused on tunnel construction), and SpaceX's Starlink project (aiming to provide global satellite internet coverage.
☛ Known for his ambitious vision, Musk has faced numerous challenges and controversies throughout his career, but he remains one of the most influential and visionary entrepreneurs of the 21st century.
